Currencies

Currency is usually a common item used to pay for rare items. These may be obtained via trading or by grinding gold in the game. Currency may be converted to another currency through simple math.

Below is a listing of the most commonly used currency in prison, which should be converted to the most common currency in prison, which is a s4 (sharpness 4 sword).

Conversion

The 5 most common currencies translated to gold amount.

  • S4 57,000

  • god set 232,360

  • obsidian x64 5120

  • ender pearl 7,125

  • snowball 114,000

Currency to currency conversion

Common ways that currencies are traded for other currencies.

  • 16 ender pearls 2 s4

  • 1 god set 4 s4

  • 11 stacks obsidian 1 s4

  • 1 snowball 2 s4
