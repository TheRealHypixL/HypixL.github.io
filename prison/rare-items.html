Rare Items

This document provides an estimated/rough price for 5 tiers of 90–98% of all existing rare items. Note that there is a wide range of prices and rarity. The tiers are based off of how expensive an item is (despite the names).

The price of rare items is based on:

  • How rare is the item?

  • How many people own the item?

  • How many of the items exist?

  • Are the origins known?

  • How cool does the item look?

  • What can you do with the item?
